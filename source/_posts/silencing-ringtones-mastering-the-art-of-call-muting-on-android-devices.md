##  Flip to Silence With the Google Phone App

 Google's Phone app comes pre-installed on Pixel devices and can be [chosen as the default dialer app](https://youtube-zero.techidaily.com/ed-in-2024-essential-guide-to-hashtagging-for-game-focused-yt-channels/) on other [Android devices](https://twitter-videos.techidaily.com/hot-takes-highlight-twitters-trending-topics-for-2024/). If you have a Pixel, the feature is called "Flip to Shhh." It mutes calls and enables "[Do Not Disturb](https://ai-video-apps.techidaily.com/new-2024-approved-transform-your-videos-with-these-12-innovative-online-animation-makers/)" when the phone is face down. On non-Pixel phones, the feature is called "Flip to Silence" and it only mutes incoming calls.

 To get started, open the "[Phone by Google](https://www.anrdoezrs.net/links/3607085/type/dlg/sid/UUhtgUeUpU206239/https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.dialer)" app and tap the three-dot menu icon in the top right.

![Tap the menu icon.](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/2022-04-15_16-47-51.jpg) 

 Select "Settings" from the menu.

![Select "Settings."](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/2022-04-15_16-48-21.jpg)

##  Flip to Silence With the Samsung Phone App

 The default Phone app on [Samsung Galaxy phones](https://instagram-clips.techidaily.com/new-connecting-consciously-with-content-craftsmen-of-your-realm-for-2024/) also has a "flip to silence"-like feature. However, it's found in the system settings rather than the Phone app settings.

 To get started, swipe down from the top of the screen to reveal the Quick Settings tiles. Tap the gear icon to open the Settings.

![Tap the gear icon.](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2021/11/2021-11-02_16-04-05.png) 

 Scroll down and select "Advanced Features."

![Select "Advanced Features."](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/2022-04-15_16-59-31.jpg) 

 Now go to "Motions and Gestures."

![Go to "Motions and Gestures."](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/2022-04-15_16-59-53.jpg)
