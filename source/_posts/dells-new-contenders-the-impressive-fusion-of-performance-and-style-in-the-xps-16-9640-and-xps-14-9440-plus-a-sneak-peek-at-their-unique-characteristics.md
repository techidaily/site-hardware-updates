With all the recent press of[issues with Intel 13th and 14th generation processors](https://tools.techidaily.com) , their failure rates, and crashing issues, it comes as no surprise that consumers will be hesitant about picking up anything with one of these affected CPUs inside, even though reports of mobile CPUs aren't affected by the same issues.
