##  Use Quick Settings to Turn On Android's Flashlight

 A quick way to activate your phone's flashlight is to [use a tile in the Quick Settings menu](https://screen-video-capture.techidaily.com/new-exploring-free-screencast-software-options-on-mac-for-2024/).

 To do that, first, pull down from the top of your phone's screen. There, tap the "Flashlight" tile (which is, unsurprisingly, the icon of a flashlight).

 If you don't see that tile, tap the pencil icon at the bottom of Quick Settings and add the "Flashlight" tile to your menu.

![Tap the "Flashlight" tile.](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/1-quick-settings-turn-on-flashlight.png)

##  Draw a Gesture to Power On Android's Flashlight

 On [Android phones that support gestures](https://review-topics.techidaily.com/in-2024-simple-and-effective-ways-to-change-your-country-on-youtube-app-of-your-tecno-spark-20c-drfone-by-drfone-virtual-android/) (like OnePlus phones), you can draw a gesture on your phone's screen to turn your phone's flashlight on and off. You'll have to first configure the gesture on your phone, as follows.

 Launch Settings on your Android phone and choose "Buttons & Gestures."

![Select "Buttons & Gestures" in Settings.](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/6-android-buttons-gestures-settings.png) 

 Select "Quick Gestures."

![Choose "Quick Gestures."](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/7-android-quick-gestures-settings.png) 

 In "Quick Gestures," from the "Screen Off Gestures" section, select the gesture you'd like to use to turn the flashlight on and off. Usually, these gestures include drawing an O, V, S, M, and W.

![Select a gesture.](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/8-choose-flashlight-gesture.png) 

 After choosing a gesture, select "Turn On/Off Flashlight."

 To disable the gesture in the future, choose the "None" option.

![Choose "Turn On/Off Flashlight."](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/9-assign-gesture-action.png)
