### Method 1: Update your drivers with Device Manager

 You can use Device Manager to update your Dell OptiPlex 7010 drivers. To do so:

**1)** Press the**Windows logo key** and**R** on your keyboard to invoke the_Run_ box.

**2)** Type “_devmgmt.msc_ “, then press**Enter** on your keyboard.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/img_5b10c441eef93.jpg)

**3)** Double click the first device type. Then right click the first device in this type and click**Update Driver Software** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/img_5b10c451b3307.jpg)

**4)** Click**Search automatically for updated driver software** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/img_5b10c46067719.jpg)

**5)**  Wait for your system to update the driver for you.

**6)** Do**step 3 to 5** for each device in Device Manager to update every device on your computer.
